Fish Recipes

Fish is healthy and easy to bake, grill, or fry.

Find hundreds of fish recipes for tilapia, cod, salmon, tuna, and more.

Fish recipes can vary widely depending on the cuisine and specific dish you're preparing.

However, here are some common ingredients often used in fish recipes:

  1. Fish fillets or whole fish (such as salmon, tilapia, cod, trout, snapper, etc.)
  2. Olive oil, vegetable oil, or butter for cooking
  3. Salt and pepper for seasoning
  4. Lemon juice or other citrus fruits for flavoring
  5. Garlic, minced or powdered
  6. Fresh herbs (such as parsley, dill, basil, thyme, or cilantro)
  7. Onions, finely chopped or sliced
  8. Tomatoes, diced or crushed
  9. Ginger, grated or minced (commonly used in Asian-inspired fish dishes)
  10. Soy sauce or fish sauce (for Asian-style recipes)
  11. White wine or dry sherry (used in sauces and marinades)
  12. Flour or breadcrumbs (for coating fish before frying or baking)
  13. Eggs (for egg wash, used before coating fish with flour or breadcrumbs)
  14. Dijon mustard or other condiments (for flavoring or as a spread)
